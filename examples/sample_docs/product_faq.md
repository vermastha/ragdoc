# Product FAQ

## Refunds

Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase. After 30 days,
refunds are issued as store credit only. Subscription plans can be cancelled
any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

## Troubleshooting

Error code XK-99 indicates a failed temperature sensor; power-cycle the device
and contact support if the error persists. Error code XK-42 means the firmware
update was interrupted — reconnect the device to Wi-Fi and the update will
resume automatically.

## Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within India. Express shipping
(2 business days) is available for an additional fee at checkout.
