# Acme Corp Employee Handbook

## Vacation Policy

Full-time employees accrue 25 days of paid vacation per year, prorated for
mid-year joiners. Vacation requests must be submitted through the HR portal at
least two weeks in advance. Unused days roll over up to a maximum of 5 days.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Fully remote arrangements require VP sign-off and are reviewed quarterly.

## Expenses

Business expenses under INR 5,000 are auto-approved with a receipt. Larger
expenses require pre-approval from your cost-center owner. Reimbursements are
processed within 10 business days of submission.
